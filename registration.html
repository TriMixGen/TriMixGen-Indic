Guidelines

  • Each team can have up to 4 participants.
  • All team members are not required to belong to same institute.
  • It's mandatory to agree to the consent form as part of the registration.
  • Upon the completion of registration, you will receive an email with dataset passwords.
  • Each team can submit up to 3 different runs and only one working note.
  • Teams must provide a detailed description of their algorithm(s) and submit their running code if required.

Reciprocal Reviewing Policy

To ensure a fair and rigorous peer-review process, we follow a reciprocal reviewing policy. Each participating team is expected to contribute to the review process by reviewing a maximum of two (2) papers assigned by the organizers. The final assignment of papers will be managed by the organizing committee while considering conflicts of interest.
